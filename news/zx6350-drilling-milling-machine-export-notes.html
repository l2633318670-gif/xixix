ZX6350 Drilling and Milling Machine Export Notes

The ZX6350 drilling and milling machine is popular because it combines drilling and milling functions in one machine. For export buyers, the important point is to confirm both machine condition and shipment preparation.

Before packing

  • Confirm spindle, table feed, and lifting movement
  • Check included accessories and tooling
  • Take clear photos of the machine from all sides
  • Protect exposed metal surfaces against rust

Loading records

Before shipment, buyers should receive packing photos, forklift loading photos, container loading photos, and final container seal photos. These images are useful for customs, insurance, and delivery confirmation.

Buyer tip

If your workshop has limited floor space, confirm machine dimensions, net weight, and unloading equipment before ordering. This avoids problems after the machine reaches your warehouse.
